About DPSA

L DPSA is led by persons with disabilities and allies. Our approach is guided by the UN Convention on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities and South Africas Constitution.

Our Mission

To advocate for inclusive laws, policies, and practices that uphold the rights of persons with disabilities, ensuring meaningful participation in all aspects of life.

Our Values

  • Dignity and Respect
  • Inclusion and Accessibility
  • Transparency and Accountability
  • Nothing About Us Without Us
