Who We Are

The Disabled People of South Africa (DPSA) is a grassroots movement committed to the social and economic empowerment of people with disabilities. We ensure that all South Africans have equal opportunities and rights.

Our Mission

To advocate for the human rights, dignity, and inclusion of persons with disabilities through lobbying, awareness, and policy reform.
